Photo Tips

Getting Started

  • Make sure the subject is in focus

  • Make sure the subject is well lit

  • Take only horizontal (sideways) photos and videos 

  • Limit extra space around subject that is not part of the main picture

  • Off-center pictures make photos more interesting

  • Subject brought forward in the frame—bit of blurred background is OK

  • Do not add graphics or word art

Recommended Camera Specs

  • Point-and-shoot digital cameras, 12 megapixels or more

  • iphone 5 or newer

  • Samsung Galaxy S7/S7 Edge & Samsung Galaxy S6/S6 Edge

  • Google Nexus 6P & Google Nexus 5X

  • LG G5 & LG G4

  • Sony Xperia Z5

  • HTC 10 & HTC One M9

  • Motorola Moto X Force

Uploading Photos to Flickr
