Healthy Community Living

Get Involved!

The Healthy Community Living (HCL) program is about all people having opportunities to live well and participate fully in their communities.

We are looking for pictures of people, with and without disabilities, at home and in their communities sharing snapshots of their day, whatever they are doing!

Help us tell the story of healthy community living by sharing goals, achievements, challenges, skills, and creative solutions that others may benefit from.

You can be part of our team by joining our Facebook page and submitting pictures of you and people you know to our Flickr page!

Pictures submitted to Flickr along with stories and video shared on Facebook can become part of our project and will become public. They will be used to help people in our communities.
