Hosting Workshops

Multimedia Presentation

Healthy Community Living workshops are ​multi-media, meaning that you will be using multiple mediums to present information, including web-based text slides, images, videos, and handouts.

​As a web-based workshop, a successful room will include the following:
​
  • An internet connection that is powerful enough to play and stream videos
  • The ability to project onto a screen or television for participants to view during the workshop​
  • Audio speakers to ensure participants can hear the videos
  • One or two facilitators who are comfortable presenting from a screen and navigating websites​​​

COOLW Guide


Download the COOLW Guide
 PDF   |   Text Only   |   Large Print​
  • Community Oriented Living Well (COOLW) is a set of guidelines designed to help plan and implement Living Well with a Disability
  • It includes ideas, agendas and procedures that community members can use to organize and conduct this health promotion workshop designed specifically for people with disabilities
  • The activities suggested in this guide may also lead community members to further address health related issues for people with disabilities in their community
