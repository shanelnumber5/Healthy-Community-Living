Purchase Healthy Community Living License
Each Healthy Community Living license you purchase allows five facilitators
​to have access to the program.

Licenses cost $500 per year and includes both the Community Living Skills and Living Well in the Community workshop and training websites as part of the Healthy Community Living program.

Multiple licenses can be purchased by the same organization.

​For questions about reduced-fee licensing visit this page.
Manage Existing Healthy Community Living License & Account
When you already have a license and need to manage it, including renewing your license or adding facilitators to the license. You may also add additional licenses if needed from your original account.
Facilitator Workshop Reporting
Please let RTC: Rural know once you have completed workshops by filling out this form. This information can help support continued funding and further development of HCL, as well as build and connect the network of HCL program providers. We will not share any identifying information to third parties outside of your organization without your consent.
