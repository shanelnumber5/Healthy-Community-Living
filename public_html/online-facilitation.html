Online Facilitation

While Healthy Community Living was originally designed to be facilitated through in-person groups using the internet to display the workshops to participants, many organizations have moved to delivering the course online using Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet or similar software.

This page has tips around acquiring and using technology, and more will be added as we learn how organizations adapt to facilitating in this way.

NOTE: Many of these resources and links will take you to outside sites that are not affiliated with HCL.

Technology and Equipment Recommendations


​We have created a short document with some recommendations for organizations looking to upgrade or set up the right computer equipment in order to more effectively provide remote service delivery.

These recommendations from RTC:Rural are not the only choices when it comes to computer purchase and set-up, but they may be a good starting place for people who have not had the opportunity to get devices for a while.
